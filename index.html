I enjoy editing photos in Photoshop, before I place them into Indesign where I can work on Desktop Publishing. When designing logos, I work on the illustrations and shapes in Illustrator to see how all the aspects work together. We learnt how to code and work in html in Dreamweaver, and I use it to create websites, as well as email signatures.

 I work in Adobe After Effects to create short animations as well as flash programs. I enjoy writing creative pieces, as well as formal essays and analysing design works to see how they portray different design styles.

I enjoy working with people as it allows me to grow and expand my skill set by learning from others. I benefit from working with clients and try to bring all their ideas to life, changing and moulding the design until they are happy.

 I love to see a design come together on a screen and work hard to make the projects I am given exactly what they were imagined to be. I draw inspiration from the everyday vibrancy of Johannesburg and try to incorporate the rushing colours and style of my surroundings into my design work.

Third Year Promotional Idea


This project's brief was for students to identify a company they would like to work for in the future,  and to design a way
to promote themselves to them.

INCORPORATING
THE IDEA



While researching places I could work, I came across Liquorice, a Digital Branding agency, which inspired me for my promotional idea. They do a lot of work on social media for digital branding. People who are part of the Liquorice design team are known as ‘Unicorns’, which are mythical and magical creatures. I found it really interesting and it inspired my use of unicorns as it links to my unique style and represents my happy, colourful character. I designed photographs
to tweet in order to get their attention digitally.
